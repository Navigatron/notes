# Summary

Take a Uniform Distro from $a$ to $b$.

Uniform Distros are symetric, therefore $E(X)=\frac {b-a} 2$

PDF: $f(x)=/frac 1 {b-a}$

CDF: $F(x) = P(X \leq x) = \int_a^x f(t)dt$

CDF: $F(x) = \frac {x-a} {b-a} \text{for} a\leq x \leq b$
